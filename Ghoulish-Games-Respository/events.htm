How to Join

  • Registration for tournaments between 6:30pm and 7:00pm, please arrive on-time.
  • New players: ask staff to help you find a table or pod.

  • Join our newsletter for reminders!

    • Store Etiquette

    • Keep language and behavior family friendly.
    • Be respectful. All players are welcome, especially new players.
    • Ask before touching someone's cards or miniatures.

  • Looking for a group? Try the Community Forum.

    • Hygiene

    For the comfort of all our guests, we respectfully request that everyone who plays at the store follows basic daily hygiene.

    We absolutely love all our gaming community members and we do not wish to embarass anyone, but we all need to be aware that our hobby involves close proximity to others.

    Please do not put us in the uncomfortable position of speaking with you about your hygiene. You may be asked to leave for the day.
