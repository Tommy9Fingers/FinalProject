Twilight Imperium: 4th ed.

This is for you strategy fans. There is a reason this is rated the 5th best overall game on BoardGameGeek.com. Diplomacy, trade, research, exploration, expansion, war. This is a space opera waiting for you to sink many hours into!

Riftbound: League of Legends TCG

The world phenomenon esports League of Legends now has a physical card game, Riftbound! This game allows for up to 4 players in a format similar to commander in Magic the Gathering. This is the hot new card game of the year; get your cards before they sell out!
