FAQ

The Frequently Asked Questions at Ghoulish Games

Do I need to be a veteran player to join events?

Nope! Our events are all beginner friendly. Our friendly staff can help you find a group who want to play with new players. We all benefit when more people get into the hobby!

How do I find people to play with?

Check out our Community-Forum for people looking for a group (LFG), or make a post yourself!

Can I bring my own games?

Yes! Bring your own games! We have dozens of tables for you and your friends to sit down and play all day! If you're looking for people to play with, check out our Community-Forum section to round up some players!

Can I get updates on the the store?

Yes! We have a Newsletter you can sign-up for that gives you updates on new games, promotions, and sales!
