About Us

A spooky gaming store for the Orland Park area & surrounding community.

Who We Are

Ghoulish Games is a Halloween-themed gaming store located in the Orland Square mall in Orland Park, IL. Veterans and newcomers to the hobby are welcome. We have a variety of board games, trading card games, and role-playing games. Be it to play games, find a group, or check out our inventory, come check us out!

We're gamers too. We want to help you find people to play with! If you have any questions, check out our FAQ or feel free to contact us!

The Store Vibe: Spooky lighting, family-friendly, all people welcome!
